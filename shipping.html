Shipping and delivery

Purchases are shipped from our respective warehouses by reputed logistics service providers. Please allow the following number of days for delivery from receipt of your order.

  • Domestic Orders – All domestic orders are processed within 3-4 business day (except for Public Holidays & Sundays). You can expect delivery of the order within 12-14 business days
  • Free Shipping
  • ORDER DELIVERIES WILL BE MADE BETWEEN 10:00 AM – 6:00 PM Monday – Saturday. Excluding public holidays
  • Goods will need to be signed for upon delivery. If you cannot be there to sign for your delivery please suggest an alternative i.e. a family member, colleague, neighbour, etc. However, Richfeel takes no responsibility for goods signed by an alternative person
